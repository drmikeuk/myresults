This means you are unable to continue on your current course and your registration with the University will be withdrawn.

We understand that this may be difficult news and want to do all we can to support you.

  • If you are eligible for an alternative award, you will be included in the next graduation ceremony, provided it is at least one month away.

Academic Appeals

  • You have 30 days to submit an appeal from the date of this message
  • Appeals are only considered if you have grounds and provide supporting evidence
  • For further information, please see Academic Appeals
  • If an appeal is ongoing, you should proceed as if the original decision stands

Extenuating Circumstances

  • If you have not previously submitted an EC, you must follow the Academic Appeals procedure
  • You will need to provide new evidence that was not, and could not have been, made available to the Board of Examiners at the time of its original decision.
