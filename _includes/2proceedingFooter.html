Next steps

Deferral of Reassessment
To opt to defer your reassessments to the next academic year, which will extend the length of your course by a year, please complete this form.
Submission does not guarantee approval, you must wait for the official outcome.

Resit Fees
If you have resits, you must pay a resit fee via NottinghamHub (available two weeks before and during reassessment). Unpaid fees will be added to your account as debt.There is no charge for first sits.

Check outstanding debts
If you have any outstanding debt, your degree will not be conferred and you cannot attend a graduation ceremony. Check your status here: Student Fees and Payments or contact the Finance Office.

Your contact information
Keep your contact information up to date through the Profile tile on NottinghamHub

Check Moodle and exam information
Use Moodle to access module content for revision and to resubmit any coursework. Check resit exam information for dates, timetables and fees and our exam period webpages where you’ll find academic resources and personal support options.

Returning to study
If you are returning from a period of interruption check your university email for a ‘return to study’ form.

Registering for the next academic session
Subject to satisfying progression requirements, you will need to register for the next academic session. Once you have registered online, you can access module enrolment to choose or change your modules if required.
