Deferral of Reassessment

  • To opt to defer take your reassessments in attendance in the next academic year, complete this form
  • Forms submitted in time will be responded to within 2 weeks of the deadline.
  • Submission does not guarantee approval, you must wait for the official outcome.
  • Important - you will extend the length of your course by a year if you defer your reassessments.

Resit Fees

  • If you have one or more resits, a resit fee is required and payable in the NottinghamHub
  • The fee will be showing two weeks before, and for the duration of, the reassessment period
  • If the fee is unpaid after resits are taken, the fee will be added to your University account
  • There is no charge for first sits

Check outstanding debts
If you have any outstanding debt, your degree will not be conferred and you cannot attend a graduation ceremony. Check your status here: Student Fees and Payments or contact the Finance Office.

Your contact information

Registration

  • You do not need to register as you are taking your reassessments not in attendance.
  • You will retain IT access and your University email, as well as access to all relevant learning materials on Moodle.
  • Contact your Module Convenor(s) if you need support with Moodle.
