Deferral of Reassessment

  • To opt to defer your reassessments to the next academic year, complete this form
  • Forms submitted in time will be responded to within 2 weeks of the deadline.
  • Submission does not guarantee approval, you must wait for the official outcome.
  • Important - you will extend the length of your course by a year if you defer your reassessments.

Resit Fees

  • If you have one or more resits, a resit fee is required and payable in the NottinghamHub
  • The fee will be showing two weeks before, and for the duration of, the reassessment period
  • If the fee is unpaid after resits are taken, the fee will be added to your University account
  • There is no charge for first sits

Academic Appeals

  • You have 30 days to submit an appeal from the date of this message
  • Appeals are only considered if you have grounds and provide supporting evidence
  • For further information, please see Academic Appeals
  • If an appeal is ongoing, you should proceed as if the original decision stands

Extenuating Circumstances

  • If you have not previously submitted an EC, you must follow the Academic Appeals procedure
  • You will need to provide new evidence that was not, and could not have been, made available to the Board of Examiners at the time of its original decision.

Reassessment/First Sit Marks

  • If you have taken a reassessment/first sit not in attendance this year, the mark will show against your module from a previous year.
  • If you have taken a reassessment/first sit in attendance this year, you may see the mark appearing in this year’s record and against your module from a previous year.
