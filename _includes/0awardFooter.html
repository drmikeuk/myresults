Check outstanding debts
If you have any outstanding debt, your degree will not be conferred and you cannot attend a graduation ceremony. Check your status here: Student Fees and Payments or contact the Finance Office.

Graduation
Everything you need to know about your graduation is on the Graduation webpages.
Your name will appear on your graduation certificate in the following format: first name(s) + family name. Check these instructions to update your name or address if needed.

If you have chosen to not attend a graduation ceremony and will graduate in-absentia, your graduation documents will be posted out to you after the graduation ceremonies to the home address as shown in NottinghamHub.
